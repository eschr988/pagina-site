Privacy Policy

Effective date: 21 July 2026

The app

Pagina does not collect any data. There are no accounts, no analytics, and no trackers of any kind.

The only network traffic the app makes is triggered by you: downloading a book or its cover from Project Gutenberg when you tap to add it from Discover. Nothing is sent to Pagina or any third party in the process.

Books you import and your reading progress are stored only on your device (and, if you use iCloud device backups, in your own backup). Pagina has no access to them.

Reading statistics and streaks are computed and stored entirely on your device as well. They are never uploaded anywhere.

This website

This website is a set of static pages. It sets no cookies and contains no analytics or trackers. It is hosted on GitHub Pages; GitHub may log technical request data (such as IP addresses) to operate the service, as described in GitHub's own privacy statement.

Contact

Questions about this policy are welcome at ios.pagina.app@gmail.com.
