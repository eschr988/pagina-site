A reading app built around one idea

Every time you pick up your phone, your book is already open. The widget shows a real, precisely measured page of the book you are reading, and the page-turn buttons work directly on the Home Screen.

  • An interactive Home Screen widget The heart of the app. Your current page, with page-turn buttons, on iOS 17 and later. Tap the text to jump into the full reader.
  • Pages that are measured, not guessed Pagina measures exactly how much text fits the widget or the reader. Your position is a precise bookmark, and progress is shown as a percentage.
  • Four classics included, seventy more to discover Start reading right away, then browse a hand-picked catalog of public-domain classics digitized by Project Gutenberg.
  • Bring your own books Import DRM-free EPUB files from the Files app, or open them straight into Pagina.
  • Typography you can tune Six classic iOS fonts, adjustable size, line spacing and margins, and a paper-and-ink look with full light and dark mode.
  • On your Lock Screen and nightstand too Lock Screen widgets show your book and progress, and StandBy keeps your page on the nightstand.
  • Reading stats and streaks, fully on your device See how much you read and keep your streak alive. Everything is computed and stored locally, nothing leaves your phone.
  • Private by design No accounts, no analytics, no trackers. The only network traffic is the book download you ask for.
