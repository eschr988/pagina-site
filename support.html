Frequently asked questions

How do I add the widget to my Home Screen?

Touch and hold an empty spot on your Home Screen, tap Edit and then Add Widget, and search for Pagina. Choose the large size (or extra large on iPad) to get the page-turn buttons. The app's onboarding walks you through the same steps.

The widget shows no text or an old page.

Open a book in the app once; the widget always follows the book you opened last. If it still looks stale, remove the widget and add it again. Widgets are refreshed by iOS, so a change can occasionally take a moment to appear.

How do I import my own books?

Pagina reads DRM-free EPUB files. Use the import button in the library, pick the files in the Files app, and they appear in your library. You can also tap an EPUB anywhere in iOS and choose Open in Pagina.

Why does my EPUB fail to import?

The most common cause is DRM (copy protection): books bought in most commercial stores cannot be opened, and Pagina tells you so. If a DRM-free file fails, please email it to us so we can fix the importer.

Can I change the font or the text size?

Yes. Settings offers six classic fonts, a text size slider, line spacing, margins, and a light, dark, or automatic appearance. Your reading position is kept when you change any of them.

Are my reading stats shared anywhere?

No. Stats and streaks are computed and stored on your device only. See the privacy policy.

How do I report a rights issue with a book?

The books in Pagina come from the public domain via Project Gutenberg. If you believe a title is offered in error, email ios.pagina.app@gmail.com and it will be reviewed promptly. See also book sources.
